## Slide 11 — Yêu Cầu Tốt Nghiệp OSSD
### *OSSD Graduation Requirements*

### Requirements
- **30 total credits** (18 compulsory + 12 elective)
- **18 compulsory credits:** English (4), Mathematics (3), Science (2), Canadian History, Canadian Geography, Arts, Health & Physical Education, French, Civics, Career Studies

- **OSSLT** (Ontario Secondary School Literacy Test) or OSSLC (Literacy Course) as alternative

4. Community involvement hours may also receive equivalencies
